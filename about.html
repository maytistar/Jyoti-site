About Jyoti

Artist. Model. Creator. Based in Patel Nagar, New Delhi.

My Journey

Two Passions, One Path

Jyoti grew up with a deep love for beauty, art, and self-expression. Based in Patel Nagar, New Delhi, she has built her career at the intersection of two worlds — as a professional makeup artist and a professional model.

As a makeup artist, she has transformed over 200 brides, worked on fashion shoots, and built a loyal clientele across Delhi NCR. Her specialties include bridal makeup, HD makeup, airbrush, party glam, and engagement looks.

As a model, she brings poise, versatility, and a strong visual identity to every shoot — from ethnic lookbooks to western fashion campaigns, brand shoot, and lifestyle photography.
