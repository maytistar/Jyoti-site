FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book Jyoti for bridal makeup?

You can book by filling the Makeup Booking form on this page or by sending a WhatsApp message to +91 99994 05631. Jyoti will confirm availability within 24 hours.

Does Jyoti travel outside Delhi for events?

Yes! Jyoti is available for destination bookings across India including Mumbai, Jaipur, Chandigarh, and more. Travel charges apply depending on location.

What makeup products does Jyoti use?

Jyoti uses high-end professional brands including MAC, Huda Beauty, Charlotte Tilbury, Airbase, and Kryolan. All products are skin-safe, cruelty-free, and long-lasting.

How far in advance should I book for my wedding?

It is recommended to book at least 2–3 months in advance for bridal makeup, especially during peak wedding season (October–February). Early booking ensures your date is secured.

Is a trial session available before the wedding?

Yes, a pre-bridal trial session is available and highly recommended. It helps finalise the exact look and ensures you are 100% comfortable on your wedding day.

What is Jyoti's rate for modeling projects?

Rates vary depending on the type of project, duration, and usage rights. Please fill out the Model Hiring form or WhatsApp directly to discuss your project and get a custom quote.
