FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book Jyoti for bridal makeup?

Fill the Makeup Booking form on this page or send a WhatsApp message to +91 99994 05631. Jyoti will confirm availability within 24 hours.

Does Jyoti travel outside Delhi?

Yes! Jyoti is available for destination bookings across India. Travel charges apply depending on location.

What makeup products does Jyoti use?

High-end professional brands including MAC, Huda Beauty, Charlotte Tilbury, Airbase, and Kryolan. All products are skin-safe and long-lasting.

How far in advance should I book for my wedding?

At least 2–3 months in advance for bridal makeup, especially during peak season (October–February).

Is a trial session available?

Yes, a pre-bridal trial session is available and highly recommended to finalise the exact look.
